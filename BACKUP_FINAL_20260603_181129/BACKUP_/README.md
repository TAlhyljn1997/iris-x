## 🎯 What IRIS-X™ Does

IRIS-X™ (Intelligent Real-time Insight System eXtended) v12.0 is a **microstructure analytics engine** that:

- Measures **market structure**, not price
- Classifies market into **6 phases** (P1–P6) based on structural signals
- Tracks **7 balance laws** across live order flow and trades
- Generates **institutional behavior patterns** (5 classifications)
- Provides **risk mapping** across 7 domains
- **Integrates** with CIE-X™ backend for macro intelligence

### The 6 Market Phases

| Phase | Name | Behavior | Trader Action |
|-------|------|----------|---------------|
| **P1** | Accumulation | Institutions buying, low volatility | **ACCUMULATE** |
| **P2** | Advancing | Rising price, growing volume, institution presence | **HOLD / ADD** |
| **P3** | Distribution | Institutions rotating positions, mixed signals | **REDUCE / PREPARE EXIT** |
| **P4** | Decline | Falling price, high volatility, distribution | **EXIT / SHORT** |
| **P5** | Capitulation | Panic selling, extreme volatility, institutional exits | **AVOID / SELECTIVE ENTRY** |
| **P6** | Systemic Fracture | Market-wide stress, cross-domain cascade risk | **RISK OFF / HEDGE** |

## 🔬 Core Concepts

### **Microstructure Balance Model™**
Quantifies equilibrium through structural field analysis across:
- **Quote Stability Index** — Bid-ask spread behavior
- **Order Absorption Ratio** — Ability to absorb large orders
- **Spoof-Cancellation Rate** — Legitimate vs. false volume
- **Cascade Intensity** — Propagation speed across domains
- **Cross-Domain Coupling** — Risk correlation between domains
- **Systemic Density** — Market concentration
- **Governance Stress** — Regulatory/intervention likelihood

### **7-Domain Activity Model**
Risk classification across:
1. **CP** (Chokepoint) — Critical flow bottlenecks
2. **EN** (Energy) — Commodity/energy sector
3. **KE** (Key Economic) — GDP, employment, rates
4. **MR** (Market Risk) — Equity volatility, crashes
5. **RG** (Regulatory) — Policy/intervention risk
6. **AL** (Allocation) — Portfolio rebalancing flows
7. **COM** (Commodity) — Raw material prices

### **Institutional Behavior Patterns**
5 classifications based on volume + flow + price:
1. **Quiet Accumulation** — Methodical entry, low visibility
2. **Aggressive Accumulation** — High conviction, rapid entry
3. **Rotation** — Sector rotation, mixed flows
4. **Orderly Distribution** — Systematic exit, risk management
5. **Panic Exit** — Forced liquidation, loss realization

## 🔍 Key Metrics Explained

### **Phase Confidence Score**
0–100% probability that the current market is in the detected phase.
- **80–100%** = High confidence, strong signal
- **60–79%** = Moderate confidence, consider with caution
- **<60%** = Low confidence, wait for clarity

### **Balance Index**
Composite score (0–100) of structural equilibrium.
- **>75** = Stable, institutional presence strong
- **50–75** = Transitioning, watch for phase shifts
- **<50** = Stressed, cascade risk elevated

### **Systemic Risk Score**
Aggregate risk from all 7 domains.
- **Green (0–30)** = Stable
- **Yellow (31–60)** = Caution zone
- **Red (61–100)** = High risk, systemic concern
