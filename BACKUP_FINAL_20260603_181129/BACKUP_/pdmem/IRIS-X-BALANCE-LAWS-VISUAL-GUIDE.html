📚 What Are the Seven Balance Laws?

The Seven Balance Laws quantify distinct aspects of market microstructure. Together, they form a holistic measure of structural equilibrium and institutional control.

1️⃣ Liquidity Law (L)

Measures execution ease and cost without distorting price.
0–30: Illiquid
31–60: Moderate
61–100: High
PLS L = 46
→ Below average, expect slippage

2️⃣ Flow Law (F)

Tracks institutional vs retail order flow direction.
<–50: Selling
–50 to +50: Neutral
>+50: Buying
PLS F = +89
→ Strong institutional accumulation

3️⃣ Risk Law (R)

Quantifies volatility regime and price uncertainty.
<40%: Low Vol
40–60%: Moderate
>60%: High Vol
PLS R = 51
→ Moderate volatility (40.8% annualized)

4️⃣ Time Law (T)

Evaluates price alignment with moving average cycles.
<40: Downtrend
40–70: Rotation
>70: Uptrend
PLS T = 80
→ Close > SMA20 > SMA50 > SMA200

5️⃣ Possession Law (P)

Quantifies ownership shift from retail to institutional.
<–50: Retail
–50 to +50: Balanced
>+50: Institutional
PLS P = +88
→ Institutions control ownership

6️⃣ Impact Law (I)

Measures price response to trade volume and pressure.
<30: Low Impact
30–60: Moderate
61–100: High Impact
PLS I = 94
→ Aggressive institutional buying

7️⃣ Authority Law (A)

Evaluates venue dominance and market control.
<40: Fragmented
40–70: Moderate
>70: Dominant
PLS A = 84
→ ASX dominance (94%), transparent

🎯 Decision Matrices

✅ BULLISH Scenario

  • Flow > +50 (Institutions buying)
  • Possession > +50 (Ownership shift)
  • Authority > 70 (Venue control)
  • Impact 60–100 (Buying pressure)
  • Time > 70 (Trend up)
→ ACTION: BUY or ACCUMULATE

🔄 ROTATION Scenario

  • Flow: –30 to +30 (Mixed sentiment)
  • Possession: balanced (Retail ≈ Inst)
  • Authority 50–70 (Some fragmentation)
  • Impact 30–60 (Moderate action)
  • Time 40–70 (Transition zone)
→ ACTION: HOLD or WAIT

❌ BEARISH Scenario

  • Flow < –50 (Institutions selling)
  • Possession < –50 (Retail dominance)
  • Authority < 50 (Fragmented)
  • Impact > 80 (Selling pressure)
  • Time < 40 (Downtrend)
→ ACTION: REDUCE or EXIT

✅ Pre-Entry Checklist

Before buying, verify these criteria:

  • Liquidity > 45 (can exit if needed)
  • Flow > +40 (institutional interest positive)
  • Risk < 60% (volatility manageable)
  • Time > 60 (price above SMA20)
  • Possession > +30 (institutional lean)
  • Authority > 60 (venue clear)
  • Impact 50–80 (not yet parabolic)
✅ If ≥ 5 checkmarks → GREEN LIGHT (proceed with entry)
⚠️ If < 4 checkmarks → WAIT (better setup coming)

🚨 Red Flags & Warning Signs

Immediate Caution (Next 5–10 minutes)

❌ Flow suddenly flips negative 30+ points
❌ Authority drops below 50 (venue fragmentation)
❌ Impact spikes above 90 on down volume
→ REDUCE position by 25–50%

Session Caution (Next 1–2 hours)

⚠️ Liquidity drops below 35
⚠️ Risk exceeds 70 (intraday volatility extreme)
⚠️ Possession turning negative
→ Consider tightening stops

Structural Caution (Next 1–3 days)

⚠️ Balance Index falls below 50 for 2+ consecutive days
⚠️ Phase probability switching from P1 to P3+
⚠️ Time Law showing price below SMA50
→ Review thesis; prepare exit plan
