## 🧮 **Quick Calculation Reference**

### **Liquidity Law (L)**
- **High L:** VWAP ≈ Close, wide volume distribution
- **Low L:** VWAP ≠ Close, concentrated bidders/askers
- **Quick Check:** Is bid-ask spread < 2 cents? → L > 60

### **Flow Law (F)**
- **Positive F:** Institutional % > 75% + buy pressure
- **Negative F:** Retail unloading, institutions dumping
- **Quick Check:** Do block trades > 1K shares outnumber single lots? → F likely positive

### **Risk Law (R)**
- **Low R (< 40%):** Tight price range within day
- **High R (> 60%):** Wide swings, hard to predict
- **Quick Check:** Price range ÷ close price = volatility proxy

### **Time Law (T)**
- **T > 70:** Price > SMA50 > SMA200 (clean uptrend)
- **T = 50:** Price near SMA20 (transition/uncertainty)
- **T < 40:** Price < SMA20 < SMA50 (downtrend)

### **Possession Law (P)**
- **P > +70:** Institutions own majority → expect breakout
- **P = 0:** Balanced (retail = institutional interest)
- **P < –70:** Retail dominance → caution zone

### **Impact Law (I)**
- **I > 80:** Major price move on high volume → conviction
- **I = 50:** Moderate volume-price alignment
- **I < 30:** Lots of volume, little price impact → distribution?

### **Authority Law (A)**
- **A > 85:** ASX > 93%, Dark Pool < 3% → transparent accumulation
- **A = 60:** ASX 80%, some Dark Pool activity
- **A < 40:** Split across venues → execution fragmentation

```
Flow: Very negative (< –60)
Possession: Reversed (< –40)
Impact: Extreme (70–100, selling)
→ Capitulation buy signals emerging
```

### **P6: Systemic Fracture** (Balance Index < 30)
```
Flow: Collapse (< –70)
```
